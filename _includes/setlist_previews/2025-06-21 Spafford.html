This was the sixth show of the 2025 Summer Tour and the band's first appearance in Brattleboro. This show featured the first In the Eyes of Thieves opener since 6/11/23 (Press Room, Portsmouth, NH) ending a 190 show gap. Hollywood contained Norwegian Wood teases from Brian and Cory and an "I have no intent to harm you" sample. High Beams contained xxxx samples from Cory. Dirtbath contained Around the World (Daft Punk) teases from Cory and Drinking Out of Cups (Dan Deacon) samples from Cory.
