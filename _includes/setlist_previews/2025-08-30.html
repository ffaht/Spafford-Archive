Set 1: America (12), The Impression That I Get (3), Neutrino (20) -> Night After Night (10), Anyone (15), The Reprise (11)

This was the 35th and final show of the 2025 Summer Tour and the band's first appearance in Lake George. This was a one set show part of the Adirondack Independence Music Festival and featured the debut of The Impression That I Get (The Mighty Mighty Bosstones). Anyone contained a Drinking Out of Cups (Dan Deacon) sample from Cory.
