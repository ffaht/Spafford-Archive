Set 1: Fake the Fate (14) -> Motel 6 (8), Darkness Singularity (13) > Money for Nothing (19) -> Neutrino (3)

This was the 27th show of the 2025 Summer Tour and the final show of the leg. This was a one set show part of the JamPacked Craft Beer & Music Festival. Darkness Singularity featured Tim Palmieri (Lotus) on guitar and contained Drinking Out of Cups (Dan Deacon) samples.
